01. Assess — see the system clearly.

Discover, map, and analyse your business processes to create a targeted, evidence-grounded optimisation plan.

What it covers

Map the work as it actually happens.

Before you automate anything, you need a shared picture of what the process really looks like — including the workarounds, the exceptions, and the friction your team has learned to live with.

  • End-to-end process discovery with subject matter experts
  • Quantitative analysis of cycle time, throughput, and rework
  • Stakeholder interviews to surface tribal knowledge
  • Heatmap of automation opportunities ranked by impact
  • Risk and dependency mapping across systems
How we work

A repeatable process — built on rigour.

STEP 01

Scope

Agree the process boundaries, success metrics, and stakeholders.

STEP 02

Observe

Shadow users, sample data, and capture the real-world flow.

STEP 03

Analyse

Identify bottlenecks, failure modes, and opportunity surface.

STEP 04

Plan

Deliver a prioritised roadmap with clear ROI per intervention.

Deliverables

What you walk away with.

Every engagement produces tangible artefacts your team can act on — not just slideware.

  • Process map (current state + future state)
  • Quantified bottleneck report with cycle-time evidence
  • Prioritised automation roadmap
  • Business case with conservative & ambitious scenarios
  • Stakeholder alignment briefing pack

Documented

Every artefact captured in a shared, searchable knowledge base.

Repeatable

Methodology you can apply to the next process — and the next.

Measured
