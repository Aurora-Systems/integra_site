Our mission

Catalysts for growth in a changing landscape.

Milestones

A few moments that shaped Integra.

2001

Integra is founded

A small team in Harare with a thesis: process automation should be designed for African enterprise reality, not retrofitted from elsewhere.

2008

First banking client at scale

End-to-end customer onboarding deployed for a major retail bank — the playbook that still anchors the practice.

2015

Expanded into healthcare

Patient-Note application launched. Clinicians get time back without giving up clinical rigour.

2019

Insurance practice

Quote-to-bind and claims automation rolled out across multiple short-term insurers in the region.

2024

AI-assisted decisioning

Document intelligence and predictive routing become part of the standard delivery for new engagements.
